6 Common Layout Errors to Avoid


Poor Alignment
You should ensure consistent alignment of text, images, and other elements.


Inadequate White Space
Allow for ample white space around elements to create a balanced and visually appealing design.


Inconsistent Fonts and Styles
You should stick to a consistent set of fonts and styles throughout the layout.


Low Contrast
You need to ensure there is enough contrast between text and background colors to make the content easily readable. Test for readability under various lighting conditions.


No Visual Hierarchy
You should use visual cues such as font size, color, and spacing to establish a clear hierarchy.

br Design Not Responsive
You need to design your layouts with responsiveness in mind, considering different screen sizes and devices.
