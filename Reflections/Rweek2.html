Design Brief Questions:
A design brief is a document that provides essential information and guidance to designers before they begin a project. It typically answers questions such as:

Project Overview: What is the project about, and what are the goals?
Target Audience: Who is the intended audience or user?
Key Messages: What messages or information should the design convey?
Brand Guidelines: Are there specific brand elements or guidelines that need to be followed?
Budget and Timeline: What are the budget constraints and project deadlines?
Deliverables: What specific items or designs need to be produced?

Brainstorming Technique: Mind Mapping
Mind mapping is a brainstorming technique that involves creating a visual representation of ideas and their relationships. It starts with a central concept or theme, and branches out into related subtopics or ideas. It's a useful tool to generate and organize ideas in a non-linear fashion, encouraging creative thinking and exploration of various possibilities.
