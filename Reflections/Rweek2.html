Design Brief Questions:

A design brief provides essential information and guidance to designers before they begin a project. It typically ask questions such as:


--- What is the project about, and what are the goals?

--- Who is the intended audience or user?

--- What messages or information should the design convey?

--- Are there specific brand elements or guidelines that need to be followed?

--- What are the budget constraints and project deadlines?

--- Are there any specific items or designs requested?


Brainstorming Technique: Mind Mapping


Mind mapping is a brainstorming technique that involves creating a visual representation of ideas and their relationships. It starts with a central concept or theme, and branches out into related subtopics or ideas. It's a useful tool to generate and organize ideas in a non-linear fashion, encouraging creative thinking and exploration of various possibilities.
